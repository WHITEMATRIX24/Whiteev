FAQ SECTION
============================================
Section Title: Frequently Asked Questions
Section Subtitle: Everything you need to know about charging with WHITE EV

Q1: How do I get started with WHITE EV?
A1: Getting started is easy! Download the WHITE EV app from the App Store or Google Play, create an account, and order your free NFC card. Once your card arrives, you can start charging at any of our 10,000+ partner stations nationwide.

Q2: Is the NFC card really free?
A2: Yes! Your first NFC card is completely free with no activation fees or monthly charges. You only pay for the electricity you use when charging. Premium and Family plan members also get free card replacements if needed.

Q3: Where can I use my WHITE EV card?
A3: Your WHITE EV card works at over 10,000 charging stations across the country from multiple networks. Use our app to find nearby compatible chargers, check availability, and see real-time pricing before you charge.

Q4: What if I don't have my card with me?
A4: No problem! You can also start charging sessions directly through the WHITE EV mobile app. Just scan the QR code at the charger or select it from the map. The app works even when your card isn't nearby.

Q5: How does billing work?
A5: We charge your linked payment method after each charging session. You'll see the exact cost before you start charging, and you can track all your transactions in the app. Premium members get automatic discounts on every session.
